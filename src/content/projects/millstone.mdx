summary: >-
  A clean-room lexical retrieval crate built to test the thesis that most
  code-retrieval workloads don't need embeddings yet. Okapi BM25 hand-verified
  against computed values, a code-aware tokenizer (camelCase, snake_case,
  unicode), and a tree-sitter repo-map with a symbol table and cross-file
  references. The bench harness runs Millstone, tantivy, and SQLite-FTS5
  retrievers over SciFact and SWE-bench-lite file-localization corpora
  (fetched by xtask with checksums, never committed), scoring nDCG@10, MRR,
  and Recall@k with metrics unit-tested against known values and a
  Kendall-tau validity check versus tantivy. The fastembed+HNSW vector
  baseline is a documented placeholder deferred to bench time. Results
  pending real runs.
